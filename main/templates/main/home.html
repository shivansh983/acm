About ACME Group of Institutions

Founded in 1989, ACME Group of Institutions has been empowering students in the fields of Industrial Accounting, Taxation, and Job Placement Assistance. Our legacy of excellence spans over three decades, making us a leading name in Haridwar and beyond.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide world-class education and services in industrial accounting, taxation, and job placement. We aim to equip our students with the skills needed to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to the business world.
